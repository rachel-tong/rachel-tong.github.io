Awards

Tong Biomedical Engineering Design Award Runner-up
May 2018

Runner-up in annual design competition among over 300 Biomedical Engineering students. The award was given for the development of a novel semi-active ankle prosthesis, which aims to capture the dynamical aspects of active prostheses while retaining the accessibility of a passive prosthesis. The device utilized pneumatic air pistons to create torque and took commands from a smartphone app to adjust the stiffness. The device was used by a transtibial amputee to perform squats during a weight-lifting routine. Patent and publication pending.
